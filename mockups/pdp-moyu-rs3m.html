MoYu RS3M v5 3x3 Magnetic Speed Cube

The MoYu RS3M v5 is one of the most popular 3x3 speed cubes in the world, trusted by speedcubers from beginners to competitive solvers. Featuring a revolutionary Maglev system combined with core magnets, this cube delivers buttery-smooth turning and incredible corner-cutting performance.

Key Features

  • Maglev System: Replaces traditional springs for a lighter, more consistent feel and virtually zero spring noise.
  • Core Magnet Positioning: Dual-adjustment system lets you fine-tune magnet strength to match your turning style.
  • UV Coating (Default): Premium surface coating that resists fingerprints and provides excellent grip in humid conditions.
  • Frosted Variant: Matte finish option for cubers who prefer a drier, textured feel.
  • Competition Ready: WCA-approved size and weight. Used in national and international competitions worldwide.
